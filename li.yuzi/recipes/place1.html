Honey Fruit Oatmeal

A great start for the day! To make a perfect bowl of oatmeal you need to prepare the following ingredients: Old fashioned oats, apple, blueberry, milk, honey

1

Pour one cup and a half of oatmeal into a pot with 200ml boiling water

3

2

Stir the oatmeal slowly over medium heat of the cooktop for about 2 minute

3

3

Pour one cup of milk, or as much as you like. Stir for another 2 minutes

3

4

Turn off the heat of the cooktop. Add a spoon of honey and stir it evenly.

3

5

Pour the oatmeal into a bowl. Top with blueberries and diced apples as you like. Done!
